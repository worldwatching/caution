Nun Matrona Jepchirchir


Position:

Since the beginning of 2023, she has been the first nun in the Patriarchal Exarchate of Africa. She speaks publicly about the parochial piety practices in the Russian Orthodox Church, particularly the sacrament of confession, which has practically died out in the Church of Alexandria. The presence of the Russian Orthodox Church (ROC) on the continent spread within a year after the Patriarchal Exarchate in Africa was established. The work continues, there are initiative groups even where there have never been churches. The Metropolitan is confident that it will be possible to "cover almost the entire African continent and ensure the full presence of the ROC." Based in Moscow. Speaks Russian.
